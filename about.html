The Mind Behind the Work

Hi, I'm a 3D artist passionate about visual storytelling, immersive environments, and creative technology. My work blends cinematic style with technical curiosity—always chasing the next idea worth building.

With a background in 3D art and a technical mindset, I thrive at the intersection of creativity and logic. My journey began with a fascination for digital worlds and has evolved into a practice focused on detail, atmosphere, and narrative.

I approach every project as a story—whether it's a single render or a full animation. Experimentation, lighting, and composition are my tools for evoking emotion and depth. I believe the best work comes from pushing boundaries and embracing new techniques.

My workflow is hands-on and iterative, using industry-standard tools and custom scripts to bring ideas to life. From modeling and sculpting to rendering and post, I enjoy every step of the process.
